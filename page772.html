Sunday 28 August 2016

LOUGHMACRORY 0 -10     1 – 13 ESKRA


Seniors exit championship following a poor performance


The seniors, after a lack lustre performance in Killyclogher this evening, exited the 2016 Intermediate championship today.

Eskra opened the game with intent but the Loughmacrory defence harried and blocked superbly managing to thwart their initial forays. It was nip and tuck until a misplaced clearance from the Lough goalmouth ended in the net to give the Emmetts a lead they would not relinquish.


 Loughmacrory were expected to come out after the break with more determination and drive but this did not materialise and despite the closeness of the scores the own goal keeping the Clogher valley men ahead it was the Emmetts who held sway throughout the second half and finished much the stronger team with a deserved six points to spare.
