Saturday 15 October 2016

POMEROY 1 - 11      0 - 07 LOUGHMACRORY


Seniors look Ring Rusty


The seniors looked ring rusty against a sharper Pomeroy side who were just coming off the back of a county championship title win six days earlier.


Loughmacrory had the better of the opening exchanges as Cathal Donaghy sent over from well out on the right wing. When Niall Donaghy’s shot which came off the upright Niall Grimes grabbed the ball and turned sharply to split the posts and make it two nil after eight minutes. Eventually Pomeroy opened their account with a free after ten minutes and then levelled the game at two apiece. Pearse Grimes put the visitors ahead by the end of the first quarter but the Plunketts made it all square a few minutes later. Pomeroy were in the driving seat now and a great point from a sideline another from play on  twenty eight minutes to put them two in front at the break 0 - 05 to 0 - 03.


Pomeroy opened the second half dangerously and were through on goal but for a great block from Kevin Fullen. They did make it six to three and had gone four up before Pearse Grimes pulled one back for the St Teresa’s six minutes in. Niall Donaghy reduced the gap further but the home side then hit a purple patch with three points to leave it 0 - 10 to 0 - 05. Loughmacrory came back a converted free after Ciarán Ward was fouled and Cathal Donaghy hit his second point to leave just a kick of a ball in at. It was the county champions who finished stronger however with a point and a last minute goal to put icing on their cake.
