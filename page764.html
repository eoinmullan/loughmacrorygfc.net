Sunday 31 July 2016

LOUGHMACRORY 5 - 13              1 - 05 COOKSTOWN


Reserves walk away won over Cookstown


The reserve side scored a facile twenty points win over the visiting Cookstown Fr Rocks in Pairc an Locha today.

The game was well over as a contest at half time Loughmacrory leading by 3 -7 to 0 -1.


The second half was just five minutes old when it became obvious that Cookstown had nothing more to offer as four Loughmacrory points from Karl Logue, Ciaran Ward (2) and Daryl Kelly extended the lead to 3 -11 to 0 -1. Cookstown pulled back a couple of points but Brendan Donaghy grabbed a high ball and fed Daryl Kelly who split the posts.  A penalty goal from the Fr Rocks gave the scoreboard a more balanced look but it was short lived as Daryl Kelly effortlessly slotted over a 45 and Pearse Donaghy palmed an Enda Ward cross to the net following a good interception form Paul Donnelly.

Daryl Kelly was unceremoniously hauled down as he bore down on goal and the referee had no option but to award the penalty which Enda Ward converted to leave the final score Loughmacrory 5 -13 Cookstown 1 -05
